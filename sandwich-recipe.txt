Ingredients:
 - 2 pieces of whole-wheat bread
 - 1 piece of cheese
 - 1 piece of lettuce
 - 1 piece of tomato
 - some sauce of Bearnaise
 - little pepper

Directions:
 1. put the cheese onto one of the bread,

 2. tear the lettuce into small pieces and put them on the cheese, then add 1 piece of tomato onto it.

 3. put some sauce of bearnaise as well as little pepper to form the taste before you put the rest of the bread.

 4. cut into 2 piece diagonally and put both in bags. Done.
